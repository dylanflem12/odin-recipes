Chicken Burgah

Description

Find below the recipe to a delicious CHICKEN BURGAAHHHH

Ingredients

Seasoning:

  • 1/3 cup /80g flour (any white)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 3/4 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder

Burgers:

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 large onions, halved them sliced (white, brown or yellow)
  • 400-500g/ 14oz-1lb chicken breast (2 pieces), cut in half horizontally into 2 thin steaks
  • 4-8 slices Swiss or other melting cheese slices
  • 4 soft rolls
  • 1 avocado
  • Lettuce
  • 2 large tomatoes

Garlic Mayo:

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise (whole egg)
  • 1 large garlic clove, minced

On the Side:

  • French fries
  • Baked potato wedges

Instructions

  1. Mix Garlic Mayo ingredients in a small bowl, set aside for 20+ minutes.
  2. Mix Seasoning in a flat bowl/dish.
  3. If required, pound chicken to about 1cm / 2/5" thick. If wanting to be exact, trim to shape of rolls BUT ensure it is about 15% larger (it shrinks).
  4. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a skillet over high heat (if doing on BBQ, drizzle flat plate).
  5. Add onion and cook, turning regularly, for 5 minutes until golden. Season with salt and pepper, toss, then remove.
  6. While cooking the onions, preheat oven to 160C/320F. Split rolls, then lightly toast/warm the rolls (if using BBQ, just toast the cut side until crispy). Leave in turned off oven with door ajar while you cook chicken.
  7. Heat 1 tbsp oil into the same skillet, still on high heat.
  8. Dredge chicken in Seasoning, shake off excess, then place in skillet. Repeat.
  9. Cook for 2 1/2 minutes until golden, then flip.
  10. Top with cheese then leave for 1 1/2 minutes. When the underside is golden, if the cheese is not yet melted, place lid on for 15 seconds (or tray or foil).
  11. Remove chicken from skillet onto plate. Cover loosely with foil while you start assembling the burgers.

Burgers:

  1. Smear base of roll with avocado, sprinkle with salt and pepper. Top with chicken, onion, lettuce then tomato.
  2. Smear lid of roll with Garlic Mayo, place on burger, Enjoy!
