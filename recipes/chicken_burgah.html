Chicken Burgah

Description

Find below the recipe to a delicious CHICKEN BURGAAHHHH

Ingredients

Seasoning:

  • 1/3 cup /80g flour (any white)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 3/4 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder

Burgers:

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 large onions, halved them sliced (white, brown or yellow)
  • 400-500g/ 14oz-1lb chicken breast (2 pieces), cut in half horizontally into 2 thin steaks
  • 4-8 slices Swiss or other melting cheese slices
  • 4 soft rolls
  • 1 avocado
  • Lettuce
  • 2 large tomatoes

Garlic Mayo:

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise (whole egg)
  • 1 large garlic clove, minced

On the Side:

  • French fries
  • Baked potato wedges
